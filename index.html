About Me

Born and raised in Orlando, I am currently an 8th grade English teacher at a college preparatory school and looking forward to retiring from education in June. Before teaching, I worked in advertising and event planning following my graduation from UF in 1999. I also had a home organization business for many years when I started teaching. I am familiar with learning management systems (LMS) and Adobe products; however, web development and coding are new to me.

Interests

Career Goals

Working remotely and maximizing my analytical and creative skills are critical to my career goals. Working remotely during the pandemic highlighted a disbalance in my day-to-day work environment, and I am seeking a more fluid and flexible approach to working. Web design and development appealed to me for many reasons; however, I understand that I have a great deal to learn to succeed in this career.

I am confident that I will quickly catch on and learn this new "language" of web design and development. I am interested in learning more about UX and SEO. While transitioning from the education sector to web design and development, the following are highlights of my current career goals:

  1. Work remotely with flexible scheduling.
  2. Work in an environment that maximizes my analytical and creative skills.
  3. Develop critical skills needed for successful web design and development.
